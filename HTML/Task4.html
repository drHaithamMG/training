The Journey to the Lake

I still remember going fishing with my grandfather early in the the morning to the lake. He would wake me up before daylight and we would hop into his old ford truck. The morning breeze would gently wake me up by the time we arrived at the lake. Once there,he would secure my life-vest and we would jump into this silver boat and head out to his favorite stop. I don't remember the conversations that much, but I always enjoyed it just being the two of us and catch the fish.

The Cooking

No matter what we caught,large or small my grandmother would clean it,season it and habe it fried by lunch time.I wish I had asked her about recipe then,but hindsight is always 20/20. This is why I figured I would merge my new programming skills and create a recipe using HTML list.
